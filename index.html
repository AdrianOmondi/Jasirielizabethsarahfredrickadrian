About Us

Jasiri Community Based Information Center

We create awereness on alternative energy to people living in rural areas. Consumers lack access to information on the various alternatives of energy available to them, the negative effects the unclean types have on the environment and how they can efficiently make use of energy thus reducing the negative effects of energy wastage. We therefore felt the need to create a community center to educate the community.

Services

Localized community based education

Educating community at the center

Employing agents to spread information

Employing people for example influencers to spread info door to door

Collaborative partnerships with companies

Patnering with companies to provide affordable solutions to the people in rural areas

Target Customers

Customers Rural households ● They do not have access to information that is readily available due to a number of reasons ● They are compromised in terms access to energy ● They are likely to make use of alternative sources of energy due to availability of space ● Pain: Isolation/exclusion/limited in terms of options ● Feeling; despair, frustration, stuck ● Coping mechanism: over reliance on the available sources, derived traditional ways of consuming solar, over pricing of energy sources ● Impact on lives: disadvantaged girls/women, over priced energy sources ● This is a year long problem that they face day in day out
